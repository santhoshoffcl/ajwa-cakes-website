FROM FACTORY TO TABLE

Our Quality Process Journey

Step inside our production workflow and see how we assure FMCG-grade perfection for every single batch.

STEP 01

Ingredient Selection

Our journey begins with raw materials. We source zero-adulteration cocoa, wild-harvested honey, certified vegetable fat, and premium flour. Every single batch is checked in our lab for density and moisture prior to mixing.

  • Laboratory purity testing
  • Clean sourcing from verified farms
  • Strict allergen isolation
STEP 02

Automated Baking Process

We cook in computerized high-efficiency tunnel ovens. This ensures uniform temperature across the top and base, preventing over-baking and locking in the internal crumb moisture for that signature melt-in-the-mouth texture.

  • Computer-controlled thermal curves
  • Touch-free food preparation pipelines
  • Perfect crumb hydration control
STEP 03

Rigorous Quality Inspection

Every single cake undergoes laser weight check, metal detection, and aesthetic inspection. Baked goods failing to meet height, moisture, or surface texture criteria are automatically sorted out by our pneumatic rejects line.

  • Inline metal detection safeguards
  • Auto-scale weight verification
  • Colorimetric baking profile checks
STEP 04

FMCG Hygienic Packaging

We pack cakes in nitrogen-flushed wrapper films with UV barriers. This retards oxidation, locks in the fresh baking aroma, and guarantees structural stability during bulk logistics transit and long storage shelf life.

  • Modified Atmosphere Packaging (MAP)
  • Sealed moisture-vapor barrier films
  • Individual flow-wrapped freshness
STEP 05

Reliable Distribution

Our Chennai factory-owned fleet and trusted distributor partners ensure the products are loaded and dispatched daily. From supermarkets to local stores, AJWA Cakes are available fresh at the counter.

  • Daily fresh dispatch cycles
  • Traceable batch barcodes
  • GPS-monitored logistics fleet
